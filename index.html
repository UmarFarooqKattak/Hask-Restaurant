About Us

Hask Restaurant offers a unique culinary experience with multi-cuisine dishes prepared by our expert chefs.

Located on Nawazish Ali Khan Road near Roots School, we pride ourselves on providing a family-friendly atmosphere where you can enjoy delicious food made from the freshest ingredients.

Our menu features a wide variety of dishes, from traditional Pakistani cuisine to international favorites, ensuring there's something for everyone to enjoy.
